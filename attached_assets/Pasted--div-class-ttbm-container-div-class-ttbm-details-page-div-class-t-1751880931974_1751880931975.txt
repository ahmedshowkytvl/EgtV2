Enjoy a full-day boat trip with snorkeling, a quad bike desert safari with Bedouin dinner and show, and a Soho Square city tour. All excursions are conducted in air-conditioned vehicles. Sharm El Sheikh airport transfers are included. Prices cover all taxes and service charges for a hassle-free experience.</p>
